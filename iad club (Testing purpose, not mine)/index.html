About

IAD Club is a tech-driven forward thinking CCA in College Central. Our mission is to ensure that our members are fully stacked with the abilities, capabilities and softskills to secure placements in the next level of their academics.

What to Expect?

You can expect to discover how the programming that we learn in class can be directly applied to solve real world problems in our everyday lives. For the curiouser and adventurous, be prepared to innovate and enhance your existing skillsets with readily available projects in either programming, design or business (if you have a project you would like to initiate, we are delighted to hear from you!).

Why should you join?
  • Secure an internship for Industry Attachment
  • Find out how to make programming exciting
  • Upgrade your portfolio
Who can join?
  • IAD club is open to all students of IAD courses
