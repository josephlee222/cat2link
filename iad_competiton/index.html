Our values and mission

Our CCA values that we strive to serve

Our mission

Our mission is to be ITE's College Central most student centric CCA Club. This is what unites all IT Applications students and advisors as we strive to enrich, execute and dream the impossible and pursue the impossible.

Cohesive Family Culture

Where Friends become Family. Cohesively care and look out for one another, so that no one is left behind. We speak freely with confidence, and others will listen with an open mind and heart.

Zeal Of Heart

Every action always comes from our very own sincere heart and having passion in what we do. If it is not from the heart, it is nothing.

Deeply rooted through all adversaries

As representatives of the club, we want to ensure that we stand firm to our objectives and not let others take us down for the sake of planning the best experience for our members even through the highs and lows.
